Priorities

Key policy priorities guiding the Square Country Government's work for its citizens.

Economic Stability: Ensuring sustainable growth and fiscal responsibility across the nation.
National Security: Protecting the country, its citizens, and interests at home and abroad.
Public Infrastructure: Investing in transportation, utilities, and public facilities for all citizens.
Education & Workforce: Improving education access, quality, and employment opportunities.
Healthcare & Public Safety: Strengthening public health systems and emergency services.
Environmental Protection: Implementing sustainable policies to preserve natural resources.
Technology & Innovation: Encouraging digital transformation and innovation across sectors.
Immigration & Customs: Effective, fair, and secure management of borders and immigration policies.
