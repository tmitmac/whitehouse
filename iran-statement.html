Prime Minister Ryan Parker releases statement on escalating Iran war

Prime Minister Ryan Parker has issued an urgent statement addressing the rapidly escalating conflict involving Iran, calling for immediate de-escalation and a return to diplomatic dialogue. The statement comes amid growing international concern over regional stability and the risk of broader global implications.

The Prime Minister emphasized Square Country’s commitment to peace, urging all parties involved to exercise restraint and prioritise civilian safety. He reaffirmed support for international law and stressed the importance of coordinated efforts with allied nations to prevent further escalation.

“My Government will always be prepared to assist the U.S. in any military operations,” the Prime Minister stated, signalling a strong alignment with key international partners while maintaining a focus on stability and collective security.

Government officials confirmed that diplomatic channels remain active, with Square Country engaging in discussions with key partners to assess potential humanitarian responses and security implications. Contingency planning is also underway to ensure the safety of citizens abroad.

The White House of Square Country reiterated its position that lasting solutions must be achieved through negotiation rather than conflict, highlighting the nation’s role as a steady and responsible voice in international affairs during a time of heightened tension.
